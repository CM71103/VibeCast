# VideoProduction Skill

## Purpose

This skill provides the VibeCast scriptwriter agent with expert-level
knowledge for creating engaging video scripts optimized for audience
retention across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

## Hook Writing Framework

Every video must open with one of these proven hook types:

### 1. Curiosity Gap
Open with a provocative question or incomplete statement that
compels the viewer to keep watching.
- "What if I told you that [topic] is completely wrong?"
- "There's a secret about [topic] that nobody talks about..."

### 2. Bold Claim
Start with a surprising statistic or counterintuitive statement.
- "This will change everything about [topic] in the next 12 months"
- "[Statistic] — and here's why that matters to you"

### 3. Story Entry
Drop the viewer into the middle of an action or narrative.
- "It was 3am when [dramatic event happened]..."
- "Nobody expected [this outcome] — here's what went wrong"

### 4. Direct Address
Speak directly to the viewer's pain point or desire.
- "If you've been struggling with [problem], watch this"
- "Stop doing [common mistake] — here's what works instead"

## Retention Curve Optimization

### The 4-Act Structure (for 60-120 second videos)
1. **Hook** (0-5s): Grab attention immediately
2. **Context** (5-15s): Set up the problem or topic
3. **Value** (15-50s): Deliver the core content in segments
4. **CTA** (50-60s): Clear call-to-action

### The 7-Act Structure (for 3-10 minute videos)
1. **Hook** (0-10s): Attention grabber
2. **Setup** (10-30s): Context and stakes
3. **Rising Action** (30s-2m): Build understanding
4. **Midpoint Twist** (2-3m): Surprising insight or pivot
5. **Deep Dive** (3-6m): Detailed exploration
6. **Climax** (6-8m): Key revelation or conclusion
7. **Resolution + CTA** (8-10m): Wrap up and next steps

## Visual Direction Guidelines

### Camera Prompts for Kling AI
When writing visual prompts for AI video generation:
- **Be specific**: "Close-up of hands typing on a keyboard with
  blue LED backlighting" not "someone typing"
- **Include motion**: "Slow zoom out from circuit board to reveal
  full data center"
- **Set mood**: "Warm golden hour lighting, shallow depth of field"
- **Specify style**: "Documentary style, 4K cinematic quality"

### Scene Transitions
- **Cut**: For fast-paced, energetic content
- **Fade**: For emotional or contemplative moments
- **Dissolve**: For showing passage of time
- **Zoom**: For revealing details or big-picture context
- **Wipe**: For comparing before/after

## Platform-Specific Formatting

### YouTube (16:9)
- Optimal length: 8-12 minutes for algorithm favor
- Strong thumbnail concept in script
- Mid-roll ad break considerations at 4-5 minutes
- End screen CTA with subscribe prompt

### TikTok (9:16)
- Maximum impact in first 1 second
- 30-60 second optimal length
- Text overlays are essential
- Trending audio integration notes

### Instagram Reels (9:16)
- 30-90 seconds optimal
- Visually striking opening frame
- Caption-friendly (many watch without sound)
- Carousel potential for detailed topics

## Narration Voice Guidelines
- Use conversational, energetic tone
- Short sentences (15 words max per sentence)
- Pause markers with ellipses for dramatic effect
- Questions to engage viewer ("But wait... what if...")
- Emphasis markers with CAPS for key words
